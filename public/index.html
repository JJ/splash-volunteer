Read about this

This is an experiment in distributed evolutionary computation where you're contributing by just visiting this page. You can read about it in this paper. Your browser will be evaluating a population of chromosomes, mutating and mixing them to create a new population in an evolutionary algorithm that sends every n generations the best chromosome to the server, receiving a random one.
